  • Link to Free People product page

    • Triggers

    hover over/off

  • User hovers over or off the over the product image.
  • User hovers over or off the heart button.
  • User hovers over or off the "Quickshop" button.
  • User hovers over or off the size buttons.
  • User hovers over or off the "Add to Cart" button.
  • User hovers over or off the "View Full Details" button.
  • User hovers over or off the close button.

    • click

  • User clicks on "Quickshop" button.
  • User clicks on the close button on modal or off of the "Quickshop" modal.
  • User clicks on the heart button.
  • User clicks on the size buttons.
  • User clicks on the "Add to Cart" button.
  • User clicks on the close button.

    • Rules

    hover over

  • When hovering over product image, the cursor changes to a hand pointer.
  • When hovering over product image, the image fades to a photo that highlights the product.
  • When hovering over product image, the Product Name color changes to pink.
  • When hovering over product image, a "Quickshop" button appears on the bottom of the image.
  • When hovering over product image, a Favorites button appears in the top right corner.
  • When hovering over the buttons "Quickshop" and Favorites, the cursor changes to a hand pointer.
  • When hovering over the favorites button, the heart slowly fades to a pink color.
  • When hovering over the Quickshop button, "Qickshop" slowly fades to a pink color.

    • hover off

  • When hovering off product image, the cursor changes to a pointer.
  • When hovering off product image, the image fades to more model centric photo.
  • When hovering off product image, the Product Name color changes to dark grey
  • When hovering off product image, a "Quickshop" banner disappears from the bottom of the product image.
  • When hovering off product image, a Favorites button disappears from top right corner
  • When hovering off the buttons "Quickshop" and Favorites, the cursor changes to a pointer.
  • When hovering off the favorites button, The heart slowly fades to a grey color.
  • When hovering off the Quickshop button, "Qickshop" slowly fades to a grey color.

    • Quickshop

    click on

  • A product modal opens (hard switch) displaying additional product information not seen on the product card.
  • Page behind modal is blurred/has a tan overlay
  • When clicking on "Add to Cart", the product is added to the cart on the "cart" page
  • When clicking on "Add to Cart", the text changes to "Added to Cart" in green, the button outline is green with a white fill
  • When clicking on a size, the product size is updated
  • When clicking on a size, the button changes to a black outline with white fill
  • When clicking on the close button, the modal disapears

    • hover

  • When hovering over the buttons "Add to Cart", "View Full Details", close, and sizes, the cursor changes to a hand pointer.
  • When hovering over product sizes, they change to a pink fill with a black outline
  • When hovering off product sizes, they change to a grey outline with light pink fill
  • When hovering over "Add to Cart", the button changes to a pink outline and white fill with pink text
  • When hovering off "Add to Cart", the button changes to a pink fill with white text
  • When hovering over "View Full Details", the button changes to a pink fill with white text.
  • When hovering off "View Full Details", the button changes to a pink outline and white fill with pink text
  • When hovering over the close button, the circle around the X disapears.
  • When hovering off the close button, the circle around the X appears.
  • When hovering off the buttons "Add to Cart", "View Full Details", close, and sizes, the cursor changes to a pointer.

    • Favorites

    click on

  • The heart fills to solid pink
  • The "Quickshop" button disapears
  • Product is added to a favorites list on the "favorites" page
